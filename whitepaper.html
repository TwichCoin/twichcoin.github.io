Purpose

TWICH is a community-focused token built on the Sui blockchain for experimentation and digital interaction. It is not affiliated with any streaming platform.

Token model

Total supply is 1,000,000,000 TWICH with 9 decimals. Supply is minted once and fixed permanently.

Vesting

10% of supply is placed into an on-chain vesting pool: 5% unlocks after 1 year and the remaining 5% after 2 years.

On-chain guarantees

  • Fixed supply (no further minting)
  • Immutable metadata (metadata capability deleted)
  • Deterministic vesting enforced by contract logic
