Mission

TWICH provides a simple and fair way to create, share, and recognize value around digital content and its creators. The project is creator-first and community-driven: creators produce value through their work, and communities should have a clear way to acknowledge that value.

Philosophy

  • Creator-first: creators deserve recognition for the value their content brings.
  • Fair: predictable rules designed to avoid surprise changes.
  • Simple: minimal complexity and clear purpose.
  • Community-driven: communities decide how value is shared and recognized.

What “value” means

“Value” can mean appreciation, recognition, support, or any community-defined signal around content. TWICH does not require a single platform, format, or use case — the intention is to enable communities to build and adopt whatever creator-support patterns make sense to them.

Use cases

  • Community recognition and appreciation around content
  • Creator support and reward experiments
  • Participation signals for digital communities
  • Builder integrations: tools that help creators and communities interact

Distribution & Vesting

The total fixed supply of 1,000,000,000 TWICH is allocated to support creators, communities, and long-term ecosystem development.

  • Creator allocation (10%)
    • 10% of the total supply is allocated to the project creators.
    • This portion is subject to time-based vesting enforced on-chain:
      • 5% unlocks after 1 year
      • 5% unlocks after 2 years
    • Vesting timestamps are publicly verifiable and designed to align long-term incentives.
    • Vesting start date: Vesting is initiated by an on-chain transaction at the start of Phase 3 (community activation). From that point forward, all unlock timestamps are immutable and publicly verifiable.
  • Community & engagement allocation (90%)
    • The remaining 90% of the supply is intended to support creator communities and participation-driven distribution models.
    • Proof of Engagement (planned — 60%)
      • 60% of the total supply is reserved for future distribution through the Proof of Engagement mechanism.
      • The Proof of Engagement system is still under design. Tokens reserved for this purpose remain under project control until the mechanism is implemented, documented, and reviewed.
    • Early community distribution (30%)
      • 30% of the total supply is allocated for early community distribution and experimentation. This may include faucets, limited participation programs, or other lightweight onboarding mechanisms for early users and creators. The Early Community allocation is custodied in the Admin/Treasury wallet and released to distribution contracts in fixed tranches of 1% of total supply (10,000,000 TWICH). Each tranche represents a discrete activation event, allowing distribution mechanisms to be evaluated before subsequent releases. This staged model is intended to reduce bot exploitation, limit sudden supply exposure, and support sustainable ecosystem growth. All release transactions are publicly observable on-chain, and detailed distribution rules will be published prior to activation.
      • Exact rules and tooling will be published before activation.
  • Transparency and guarantees
    • TWICH has a fixed total supply with no future minting.
    • All allocations and vesting schedules are enforced or observable on-chain.
    • Distribution mechanisms will be published before use.
    • No guarantees of rewards, returns, or value are implied.

Proof of Engagement

Proof of Engagement is a mechanism for distributing TWICH based on measurable signals of audience attention and interaction. Rather than fixed payouts, rewards are assigned through a weighted and probabilistic process, where higher verified engagement increases the likelihood of receiving tokens. This approach allows recognition to emerge organically from real participation, while remaining flexible, platform-agnostic, and open to community-defined rules and tooling over time.
